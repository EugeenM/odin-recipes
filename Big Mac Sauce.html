Big Mac Sauce

Description

Sweet, Spicy, Decadent Sauce similar to McDonalds Big Mac Sauce

Ingredients

  • Mayo
  • Ketchup
  • sugar
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Peprika

Steps

  1. add 30g of Mayo, 30g of Ketchup
  2. add two tablespoons of sugar garlic and onion powder
  3. Finish with three table spoons of Peprika
  4. mix everything together until smooth consistency
