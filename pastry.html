Puff Pastry

Description

Rich, Flakey, Puff Pastry stuffed with minced beef and onions.

Ingredients

  • Puff Pastry Sheet
  • 30g minced beef
  • carmalized onions
  • Butter
  • Egg wash

Steps

  1. Prepare the beef first by seasoning and cooking,
  2. On a seperate pan prepare the carmalized onions with sugar
  3. Combine both once fully cooked
  4. Cut the pastry sheet in two and use egg wash on the sides so that we have a proper seal then place the beef inside
  5. Seal the pastry and butter the outsides
  6. Bake for 10-12 mins and enjoy
